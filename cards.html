Simple game that gives you a chance to win crypto everyday!

IDEA

  1. Register your account with a valid email (If you win, we will contact you through the email to ask for your USDT address).
  2. Due to this game being a prototype, we will currently allow up to 250 users only.
  3. The game will reset daily at 8:00 a.m. GMT+8, where each player will have a balance of 100 gold.
  4. There will be a list showing all the players and their gold, in descending order of their gold
  5. Players can send another player gold by clicking on their names
  6. Each transaction can contain 1-10 gold (without decimals) and has a cooldown duration of 1 minute
  7. The player with the most gold at the end of the day (12 a.m. GMT+8) will receive an email asking for their USDT TRC address.
  8. The USDT will be sent within 48 hours upon receiving their reply.
  9. Due to the limited number of players available, players who are inactive for 2 days will have their accounts deleted (requires re-registration)
